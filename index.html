You Can't Play Music

But you can start right now.

Wear headphones. Use a keyboard.

Start by creating the first note of a new instrument.

Volume

Press the "o" key. The tone gets louder (darker blue). The "n" key softens the volume.

I've arranged the keys in the fastest typing position. Every stroke matters. Follow my directions.

Time

The "h" and "l" keys move the red cursor horizontally. Time moves across the squares from left to right.

Tone

You're hearing a C# in the 3rd octave. Don't move your hands away from typing position.

To change a different tone, use "j" to move down then "k" to move up. There are 12 tones in each octave.

C - C# - D - D# - E - F - F# - G - G# - A - A# - B

Octave

You can scroll past the bottom of an octave. Use the "j" key. You are now in the 2nd octave.
